How FlashFlow works

Import: one CSV becomes one deck. FlashFlow supports front/back, question-bank style Stem/Answer/Explanation, and text entries with [[answer]] markers.

Review flow: reveal the answer first, then rate the card with Again / Hard / Good / Easy. Those ratings drive the spaced-repetition schedule, so they matter more than speed.

Typing mode: if enabled, you type your answer before revealing. You can keep grading manually, or turn on Auto-rate typed answers if you want FlashFlow to estimate the rating from how close your answer was.

Deadline planner: optional. If you set a target deadline, the planner suggests recommended study sessions in the side panel. Without a deadline, the app stays on normal spaced review.

Side panels: use the six-dot drag handles to reorder the left and right side panels however you want. The order is saved locally on your device.

Deck control: each deck can be active, inactive, studied solo, renamed, exported, or deleted. Only active decks contribute cards to your queue.

Keyboard shortcuts: Enter = reveal in typing mode · Space = reveal · 1 / 2 / 3 / 4 = rate.
