Quick guide before you start

This tool works best when you first set up your categories and weights, then enter your Max and Score values carefully. The calculator is fast, but your results still depend on the numbers you enter.

Step 1 Add your subject

Each subject is like one class in school. You can make many subjects and switch between them in the left panel using the subject list.

Important Set your categories

Categories are the parts of your grade, like quizzes, homework, participation, and exams. Their percentages must add up correctly so the final grade is fair. If the weights are wrong, the summary will not be reliable.

Step 2 Enter scores

For every item, put the highest possible score in Max and your actual score in Score. The score sheets update live as you type.

Base Grade Understand transmutation

This tool can raise raw percentages using a base grade. Even if the raw score starts low, transmutation can convert it into the grading system your school uses. The highlighted Score (B40) values show that adjusted result.

Prelims Midterms Finals See your term grades

The tool computes your Prelims, Midterms, and Finals raw grades. It also combines them into your Midterm Grade and Final Grade using the base grade/transmutation settings.

Goal Mode Set a target

If you want a target final subject grade, the tool can estimate what Finals raw grade you need to reach it.

Tips Edit setup faster

In Setup, click the category name or weight to edit them quickly. You can also use the drag handles to reorder categories and subjects when needed.
