How this tool works
Simple guide for students

1. Add your subject

Each subject is like one class in school. You can make many subjects and switch between them in the left panel.

2. Set your categories

Categories are the parts of your grade, like quizzes, homework, participation, and exams. Their percentages must add up correctly so the final grade is fair.

3. Enter scores

For every item, put the highest possible score in Max and your actual score in Score.

4. Understand transmutation

This tool can raise raw percentages using a base grade. For example, even if raw score starts low, transmutation can convert it into the grading system your school uses.

5. See your term grades

The tool computes your Prelims, Midterms, and Finals grades. It also combines them into your Midterm Grade and Final Grade.

6. Use Goal mode

If you want a target final grade, the tool can estimate what Finals grade you need to reach it.

7. Edit setup faster

In Setup, click the category name or the percent badge to change them quickly.
